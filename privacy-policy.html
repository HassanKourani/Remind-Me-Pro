Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 30, 2026

RemindMe Pro ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address: Used for account creation, login, and password recovery
  • Display name: Optional, used to personalize your experience

Reminder Data

  • Reminder titles, notes, and settings you create
  • Categories and priority levels you assign
  • Scheduled dates and times

Location Data

  • Location data is collected only when you create location-based reminders
  • We store location coordinates to trigger reminders when you arrive or leave specified places
  • Location access can be revoked at any time through your device settings

Device Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Anonymous usage analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the reminder service
  • To sync your reminders across devices
  • To send reminder notifications
  • To trigger location-based reminders
  • To process subscription purchases
  • To improve our app and user experience

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Secure cloud database and authentication
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management
  • Google AdMob: Advertising (for non-premium users only)
  • Expo: App development and notifications
Note: Premium subscribers do not see any advertisements. Ads are only displayed to free users.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • Data is stored on secure cloud servers
  • We use industry-standard security practices
  • Local data is stored securely on your device

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share data only:

  • With service providers who assist in operating our app
  • If required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights or safety

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-out: Disable location services or notifications
Delete Your Account: To request deletion of your account and all associated data, please visit our Account Deletion Page.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law.

8. Children's Privacy

RemindMe Pro is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
